Add Containers


Count the bulk number of skids on the truck load. Enter the value in the field below. For example if there are 10 skids and each skid containing 6 plastic containers you can enter either 10 or 60. The system will loop through the total number you entered and give you the opportunity to breakdown either each skid (for further breakdown later) or each container (for breakdown now).
